## Mission
Advance critical infrastructure resilience through tightly bounded autonomous coordination while preserving explicit human governance, cryptographic auditability, and immediate revocability of every execution pathway.

## Vision
An adaptive compute fabric where high‑value analytic & simulation workloads self‑optimize for latency, energy and reliability—yet remain transparently inspectable, policy‑enforced and instantly interruptible. Intelligence as an accountable utility, not an opaque actor.

## Architecture Layers
| Layer | Purpose | Guarantees |
|-------|---------|------------|
| Ingestion Mesh | Multi‑spectrum signal & state capture | Schema integrity • temporal ordering |
| Neural Core | Bounded predictive windows & ensembles | Time‑boxed execution • deterministic rollback |
| Simulation Sandbox | Counterfactual forking & stress exploration | Air‑gap • irreversible memory wall |
| Policy Gate | Signed quorum escalation & intent filtration | Multi‑party veto • HW attestation |
| Containment Envelope | Active kill lattice & anomaly quarantine | Sub‑second isolation • trace continuity |

## Operating Principles
1. **Containment First** – Every autonomous loop has a hardware & policy kill path.
2. **Observable by Design** – Telemetry, chain hashing & signed exports default ON.
3. **Least Irreversibility** – State transitions rollback‑eligible until quorum commit.
4. **Energy Accountability** – PF/kW efficiency surfaced at pod & cluster tiers.
5. **Human Quorum** – Cross‑discipline concurrence gates escalation.

## Safety & Governance
- Hardware attestation + signed policy bundles enforce runtime invariants.
- Triple‑path veto (Engineering · Ethics · Oversight) – any single path halts escalation.
- Deterministic snapshot cadence with SHA‑256 chain + FNV fallback.
- Mesh degradation surfaced without halting unaffected lanes.

## Transparency & Compliance
- Immutable cryptographic audit chain for exports.
- Live provenance indicators (no silent model / runtime drift).
- Continuous internal red‑team & external review; autonomous kinetic authority explicitly excluded.

## Market Position
Verifiable coordination substrate for regulated, safety‑critical domains (energy, aerospace, advanced manufacturing) where opaque black‑box AI is unacceptable. Emphasis on measurability, revocability, and power efficiency.
